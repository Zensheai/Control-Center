3 mistakes that slow solopreneurs down
1
Trying to automate everything at once. Start with one task, get it working, then add the next. Compounding wins beat scattered attempts every time.
2
Expecting AI to sound exactly like you on the first try. It won't. AI needs prompting, examples, and your editing to get close — but once you train it right, it gets faster every time.
3
Buying paid tools before proving the free tier works. Claude and ChatGPT free tiers are genuinely powerful. Get your first win for free before spending anything.
Your 30-Day Action Plan
Four weeks. One task at a time. Real results.
1
Week 1 — Pick one task and prove it works
Choose task #1 from your Top 3. Use AI for that task only — nothing else. Track how long it takes before and after so you have a real number.
Don't add new tools. Use the free tier of Claude or ChatGPT. You don't need to spend money to get your first win.
2
Week 2 — Build a repeatable process
Once the first task works, write down the prompt you used and the steps you followed. Turn it into a simple checklist you can repeat every week without thinking.
A prompt that worked once will work every time if you save it. Start a simple doc with your best prompts — this becomes your personal AI playbook.
3
Week 3 — Add your second task
Now that the first task runs consistently, add task #2 from your Top 3. Apply the same process. At this point you have a two-part system saving real hours every week.
Two AI-handled tasks per week = compounding time savings. Most solopreneurs stop at one. Don't be most solopreneurs.
4
Week 4 — Review and expand
Look back at your audit. Which tasks that were "Partial" feel more manageable now? What's moved from "Not yet" to "worth trying"? Set your next 30-day target.
Your AI skills improve fast. Tasks that felt intimidating at the start of this month will feel routine by the end of it.
